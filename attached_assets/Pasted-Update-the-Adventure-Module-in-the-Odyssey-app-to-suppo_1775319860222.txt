Update the **Adventure Module** in the Odyssey app to support:

1. Media creation (upload + real-time capture)
2. Post-completion editing and reuse
3. A fully separate **Child Mode UI** (based on provided designs)
4. A gamified reward and feedback experience

---

# 🧩 1. MEDIA HANDLING (CRITICAL FEATURE)

Each Step in an Adventure must support:

## Options:

### A. Upload Existing Media

* Upload image or video from device
* Store URL in database (Supabase storage or backend)

### B. Record in Real-Time

* Use device camera (getUserMedia API)
* Allow:

  * Record video
  * Capture photo
* Save media immediately and attach to step

---

## Step Model Update

Each step must include:

* instruction
* mediaType ("image" | "video")
* mediaUrl
* thumbnail (optional)

---

## UI (Builder)

Each step card must show:

* Media preview
* Buttons:

  * “Upload”
  * “Record”
  * “Replace”

---

# 🔁 2. ADVENTURE LIFECYCLE

---

Allow:

### A. Edit Adventure

### B. Reuse Adventure
