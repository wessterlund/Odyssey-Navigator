Your task is to build and integrate the “Voyage Path” module based on the provided design.

🧠 CORE CONCEPT

A Voyage Path is a structured intervention plan for a student.

It consists of:

Explorer’s Mission (Goal)
Adventures (Tasks / Steps)
Rewards (Motivation system)
Schedule & Settings
Execution + Logging
